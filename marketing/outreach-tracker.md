## 🔄 Follow-up Strategy

### First Contact (Day 0):
- Send primary message (GitHub/Twitter/Discord)
- Keep concise, value-focused

### Follow-up 1 (Day 3):
- If no response: polite nudge
- "Just following up - thought this could be valuable for [specific feature]"
- Offer alternative contact method

### Follow-up 2 (Day 7):
- Final attempt if high-value target
- "Wanted to make sure you saw this before we reach out to similar frameworks"
- Clear close if no interest

### Positive Response:
- Schedule call within 3 days
- Send Cal.com link (https://cal.eu/joptimal/erstgespraech)
- Prepare demo + technical details
- Have bounty agreement ready
